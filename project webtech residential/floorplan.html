BHK1 Imperial Suites

A compact residential unit featuring two bedrooms, with one measuring 12' x 12' and the other 13' x 14'. The living room (13' x 14') is centrally located, furnished with a sofa, coffee table, and chairs, and connects to a kitchen that includes a refrigerator, stove, pantry, dishwasher, and stackable washer/dryer. There are two bathrooms, one near the kitchen and another next to the smaller bedroom, as well as a walk-in closet next to the smaller bedroom. A small patio is accessible from the larger bedroom, and the entrance to the unit is located at the bottom of the floor plan. This layout ensures functional separation of sleeping and living areas while maximizing space.
